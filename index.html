TEAM ANANTAM

SKY IS THE LOWER LIMIT !!


Vision:

To develop rockets, create awareness, and encourage a passion for the study of space and astronomy research.

Mission:

We aim to design and build model rockets, participating in both national and international competitions. Our team focuses on every aspect of rocketry, from design and construction to static testing, launch preparation, liftoff, and recovery.

Structure:

Our team is divided into focused units, including design, analysis, avionics and communication, propulsion, and non-technical support. With access to well-equipped labs and guidance from experienced faculty, we foster innovation and practical application of knowledge.

Objective:

We aim to deepen students' understanding of physics and engineering through hands-on rocketry and astronomy, foster innovation in aerospace and a passion for cosmos exploration, and empower students to excel in national and international contests, promoting practical knowledge application, collaboration, and excellence.

Every component and subsystem is meticulously designed, analysed, and manufactured in compliance with competition regulations, ensuring the highest standards of quality and innovation. We are committed to representing PCCOE in prestigious competitions and advancing our research and technical capabilities through collaboration with renowned teams and industry experts.
